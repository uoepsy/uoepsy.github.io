Welcome to the website of the research methods and data analysis courses provided by the department of Psychology of the University of Edinburgh.
Below you can find the lecture slides and workbooks for our three undergraduate courses (DAPR1, DAPR2 & DAPR3) and our two postgraduate courses (USMR & MSMR). During the semester, new content is made available each week, but you can find archives of each course from previous years in their entirety.
