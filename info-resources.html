General information

We offer a range of support relating to research methods for staff and students in the department of psychology.

Statistics Support for dissertations (Undergraduate)

  • During the busy undergraduate dissertation period (Jan - April) we run a Stats Clinic up to 3 times a week for Psychology UG students. These are drop in sessions during which you can ask 1-to-1 questions about your project. These are aimed to provide quick assistance for R & stats issues, enabling us to reach and assist as many students as we can. Please be aware that these are not intended to involve fully getting to grips with your project, but more aimed at providing generalisable assistance and resources for you to follow, along with help debugging code

Statistics Support for Staff and Postgraduate students

  • Our office hours throughout the year are on Wednesdays from 10 to 11am. During term time, these may be busy with students from our various courses to whom we must give priority. However, outside of term time you are very likely to be able to get hold of us during this hour to chat through any problems/code issues/questions that you may have and to point you to useful resources. We may be open to providing more in-depth support on projects, but in these cases we ask for upfront discussion on collaboration agreement.
    Currently, our office hours are taking place virtually. Please email us pg.ppls.stats@ed.ac.uk for info.
