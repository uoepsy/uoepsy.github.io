Support

Support Sessions

  • Course-related support: Please see the relevant Blackboard Learn page for details of office hours

  • Statistics support for UG & MSc Dissertations: Your supervisor should be your first port of call for any questions about your dissertation, including those about statistical methods. If your supervisor is unable to assist, please ask them to book a session with us here. Please note, students cannot book these sessions directly, and requests must go via their supervisor.

  • Statistics support for PhD students: We hold monthly sessions for PhD students to come nad chat about stats or get help with code. These take place on the first friday of every month. Please book in for a slot here.

  • Staff support: Please email us directly
